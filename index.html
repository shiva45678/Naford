About NAFORD

Since our inception, the National Federation of Resident Doctors Association has been the cornerstone of medical education excellence and resident advocacy across India's premier medical institutions.

Our Story

Born from the collective vision of dedicated resident doctors, NAFORD represents the unified voice of medical professionals across all AIIMS and INIs. We stand as guardians of medical excellence, advocates for resident welfare, and pioneers in shaping the future of healthcare education in India.

Our journey has been marked by unwavering commitment to improving working conditions, enhancing educational opportunities, and ensuring that every resident doctor receives the support they need to excel in their medical journey.

Our Mission

To champion the cause of resident doctors and elevate the standards of medical education and healthcare delivery across India.

Excellence in Medical Education

Committed to the highest standards of medical training and continuous professional development for resident doctors.

Visionary Leadership

Guiding the future of healthcare through innovative policies and progressive advocacy for resident welfare.

Compassionate Care

Fostering a culture of empathy and dedication to patient care while supporting the wellbeing of our medical professionals.
